Frame 15

The brief, in detail.

Palette

  • olive-moss
  • walnut-brown
  • brass-sand
  • pine-green
  • terracotta-warm
  • limestone-cream
  • adriatic-steel

Equipment

  • four Shimpo VL-Whisper electric pottery wheels (one per student)
  • wedging oak table, 260 × 90 cm, canvas-topped
  • top-loading electric kiln 1260°C (80 L) — cone 6
  • glaze lab: 12 dip-buckets, 3 brush jars, 1 wax-resist table
  • drying shelf — 3 tiers, 24 boards, slow-vent cover
  • apron rack — 8 linen aprons, olive-moss dye
  • central chalkboard with the morning's clay body, firing schedule, and one word
  • long pine bench for 8 with brass hooks under edge

Ritual

guest arrives 09:45 → ties on linen apron with initials → host writes 'the word of the day' on chalkboard → 10:00 wedging clay by hand on oak table → 10:30 each pair at a wheel, 50-min throw → noon break, burek + kava → 13:00 trim + sign underside → 14:30 fire in kiln → return after 4 days for glazed piece

Materials

waxed oak wedging table · brass hooks · canvas · linen aprons (olive-moss) · pine floorboards · lime-washed walls · terracotta bisque shelves · adriatic-steel kiln · hand-thrown chalk cup on the board rail

Audience

  • Zagreb locals who want one evening off screens
  • tourists from Vienna + Ljubljana on weekend trips
  • architects and designers wanting material intuition
  • small-company off-sites (up to 6) — team booking

What makes it unique

the only pottery class in Zagreb where students write their own word on the morning chalkboard, throw on Shimpo Whisper wheels in pin-drop silence, and leave with a signed, photographed, fired piece — glazed in one of three house colours only
