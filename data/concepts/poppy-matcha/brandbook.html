The Concept

Ceremonial matcha
with Memphis soul.

Specialty coffee has saturated European food courts. Matcha hasn't — yet. Poppy Matcha captures the wellness + aesthetic momentum of Gen Z consumers with a product (ceremonial-grade Japanese matcha) that commands €5 per cup at 16% COGS, wrapped in a bold Memphis-design kiosk that functions as its own billboard on Instagram.

The kiosk model skips the #1 small-business trap: committing €3,000–€15,000 to renovate a leased space you don't own. Every asset in this brandbook is physically movable. If the food court doesn't work, you pack up and re-open somewhere else the same week.

The Renovation Rule

Never spend money on walls, flooring, electrical, or plumbing that stays behind when your lease ends. Every euro in this concept is in something you can unscrew, lift, and take with you. That is why the renovation budget is €0.

Registration · Netherlands

Legal setup in 5 days.

Netherlands has one of Europe's fastest sole-trader registrations. Full legal setup: €80 + €60, same business week.

1. KvK Registration

  • Legal form: Eenmanszaak (sole trader)
  • Portal: kvk.nl/inschrijven
  • Fee: €80.10 one-time
  • Time: same-day
  • Required: passport + BSN + NL address

2. Tax (Belastingdienst)

  • BTW (VAT) number: automatic after KvK, 5 days
  • VAT rate: 9% on food & non-alcoholic drinks
  • Income tax: Box 1, progressive
  • Optional: KOR (VAT exempt if <€20k turnover)

3. Food Safety

  • Warenwet hygiene certificate: €60 online
  • HACCP self-control plan (free template)
  • GGD Amsterdam review: 1 week, free
  • Inspection: within 3 months of opening

4. Social & Insurance

  • No mandatory social security for ZZP
  • Mandatory: private health insurance ~€135/mo
  • Recommended: AVB liability (€12/mo)
  • Recommended: AOV disability (~€90/mo)

Total legal + registration costs: €350 (KvK €80 + hygiene €60 + misc filings €210)
