The Concept

Cold-pressed,
bottled at dawn.

Amsterdam has two kinds of juice: €2 sugar-spiked supermarket cartons and €9 plastic-cupped tourist smoothies. Nothing in between — a proper hydraulic cold-press in a glass bottle, same day, €6.

AJL fills that gap with eight disciplined recipes, a Goodnature M-1 single-auger press visible through the window, and a €9,800 kiosk that pays back in Month 4 without a single Deliveroo order.

The 12-Hour Rule

Every morning at 6am, the day's fruit is pressed on-site. Bottles are numbered, dated, and sold within 12 hours. Whatever's left at 18:00 goes to the staff room or the compost — never to tomorrow. No HPP, no thermal, no preservatives.

USPs & Positioning

Why this wins.

Five defensible advantages over the supermarket cartons and the tourist smoothie bars.

  1. Pressed on the window. Customers watch the Goodnature M-1 crush 40 kg of fruit at 08:00. No other Amsterdam juice bar lets you see the press.
  2. Glass, numbered, dated. Every bottle has a batch number, a pressed-at timestamp, and €0.50 glass deposit. Zero plastic, closed loop.
  3. Eight recipes, no substitutions. Menu cost control is total. Fruit order is fixed, waste < 4%.
  4. 12 m² corner kiosk. Rent €650/month in Jordaan — 60% below a café lease. The hatch is the shop.
  5. Hatch-only, no seats. No waiters, no table turn, no VAT 21% on dine-in — takeaway 9%.

Pattern Library

Zest circle.

One monogram, one stamp, zero decorative pattern. The Jordaan rule: if you can avoid a pattern, avoid it. Cream wall + leaf awning + glass bottle = pattern enough. The only repeating mark allowed is the circular batch stamp.

Monogram is used at 25 mm on bottle caps, 60 mm on awnings, and 300 mm on the hatch window. Never stretched, never rotated, never placed on a photograph.

Legal · NL

Registration checklist.

Netherlands food kiosk setup is methodical but not slow. The whole stack can be finished inside 3 weeks if you start KvK and NVWA in parallel.

Business

  • KvK (eenmanszaak or BV) — €80, same-day online
  • BTW-nummer automatic from KvK
  • ZZP without staff, or BV if partners
  • Bunq business account — €10/month
  • Liability insurance — Centraal Beheer, ~€30/month

Food + Premises

  • NVWA registration (Levensmiddelenbedrijf)
  • HACCP hygiënecode voor sappencentrales
  • Gemeente Amsterdam — horeca vergunning Cat. 3 (kiosk)
  • UNESCO Jordaan facade consent — awning colour + size
  • Waste: De Ceuvel pulp pickup contract

Tax

  • BTW 9% on takeaway juice
  • Inkomstenbelasting — quarterly ZZP-aangifte
  • Kleineondernemersregeling if < €20k turnover
  • MKB-winstvrijstelling 13.31%

Staff (from month 3)

  • UWV werkgeversregistratie
  • CAO Horeca — minimumloon 2026
  • Payroll via Employes or Nmbrs
  • Pensioenfonds Horeca (verplicht)
