What exactly do I get for €49?
A 25-page magazine-editorial brandbook PDF with palette, axonometric layout, equipment list (with real € prices), signage, menu/services grid, 4-week opening timeline, unit economics, and print-ready identity. Not generic templates — every concept is shot and costed for a specific city.
Can I really open in 4 weeks?
If you have a lease and budget approved — yes. The 4-week schedule covers contractor brief, equipment orders, signage print, soft-open. Larger projects (>50 m²) realistically need 6–10 weeks. The brandbook tells you which timeline applies to your concept.
What's the difference between €49 and €149?
€49 (Concept) — non-exclusive license. Other builders may open the same concept in different cities. €149 (Exclusive) — concept removed from the catalog forever. Editable source files. Full commercial rights transfer. Your unbuilt world, now private.
Is it a franchise?
No. You own the business, we never take equity, royalties or a brand license fee. micro.svita is a shop of visual concepts — you pay once, open the business, keep 100%.
Refunds?
14 days, no questions, full refund. You also keep the files we already sent — we don't claw them back. We'd rather lose €49 than have you feel cheated.
Can I use it in any country?
Yes. Concepts are designed EU-wide. Prices, suppliers and regulatory notes lean on the original reference city, but the visual system, economics and 4-week plan translate cleanly to any European city.
